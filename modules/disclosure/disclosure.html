Patient Questionnaire and Appointment Disclosure

Please read the information below before beginning your questionnaire.

Purpose of This Questionnaire

This questionnaire is provided to collect preliminary health information before your appointment. The information you provide helps your clinician prepare for your visit, improves the efficiency of the encounter, and allows more time to focus on the concerns that are most important to you during your appointment.

Medical Record Disclaimer

Information entered into this questionnaire is preliminary and is provided by the patient before the clinical evaluation. Patient-entered information does not become part of the official medical record unless and until it has been reviewed, verified, and incorporated into the medical record by the treating clinician.

The treating clinician retains sole professional responsibility and discretion, consistent with applicable standards of medical care, to review, clarify, revise, supplement, or exclude patient-entered information as necessary to ensure that the medical record is accurate, complete, and clinically appropriate.

The official medical record consists only of documentation that has been finalized and authenticated by the treating clinician.

Completion of this questionnaire does not constitute medical advice, does not replace the clinician's independent history, physical examination, assessment, clinical judgment, or medical decision-making, and does not establish a clinician-patient relationship beyond the scheduled healthcare encounter.

Appointment Expectations

To provide timely access to care for all patients, appointment times are generally scheduled for approximately:

  • 20 minutes for new patients
  • 10 minutes for established patients

During your visit, the clinician will prioritize your concerns based on medical necessity, clinical urgency, patient safety, and the time available.

Although every concern you identify is important, it may not be possible to fully evaluate or treat every issue during a single appointment. Additional concerns may require one or more follow-up visits to ensure that they receive appropriate evaluation and management.

Please list your concerns in order of importance, with your most important concern listed first.

If you have a concern that you believe requires immediate medical attention, please notify the office staff before completing this questionnaire.

Medical emergencies: This questionnaire is not intended for reporting medical emergencies. If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department immediately.
